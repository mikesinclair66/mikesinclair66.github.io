Tyler Niles is the owner and operator of Niles Mobile Repair, working fulltime on heavy duty Mercedes Benz Unimogs, Agricultural equipment and other European off-highway vehicles. He has completed his Automotive Red Seal certificate as well as various business courses. Creating good relationships with customers is a quality he values most with his company.

Tyler is a follower of Christ and leads his day through Him. He has been married to his wife Alyssa for almost seven years and they're raising their two sons in Mission, BC.

He has been a member of Mission Search and Rescue for just over 3 years and has completed several training courses for his volunteer work, most recently being the First Responders First Aid course.

He loves to spend time with his family, play baseball, be surrounded by friends, go hunting, hiking, fishing, and loves exploring this beautiful province.

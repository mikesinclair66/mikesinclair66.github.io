What is the Fraser Valley Rally?

The Fraser Valley Rally with Max Bernier is hosted by the Fraser Valley PPC Electoral District Association and the respective EDAs and Official Agents for several PPC candidates running for election in the Fraser Valley and its neighbouring districts!

They are as follows:

  1. Kevin Sinclair for Abbotsford
  2. Rob Bogunovic for Chilliwack-Hope
  3. Tyler Niles for Mission-Matsqui-Fraser Canyon
  4. Rayna Boychuk for Langley-Aldergrove
  5. Ian Kennedy for Cloverdale-Langley
  6. Juliuss Hoffmann for Pitt Meadows-Maple Ridge
  7. Jennifer Singh for Steveston-Richmond East
  8. Pamela Singh for Surrey-Newton
  9. Amrit Birring for Fleetwood-Port Kells
  10. Joe Kennedy for Surrey Centre

About Us

Welcome to Bass RV, where we would love to introduce to you the beautiful Judson Lake owned by Walter and Anne Willms. Our lake is large and full of bass, and our property offers a great RV campsite. With our catch and release policy, our lake is a great place to polish your fishing skills.

If you are looking for somewhere you can camp and fish, give us a call! We offer three RV sites with 30 ANP service, water, and sewer hook-up. For more information, you can contact us via the "Contact Us" page.

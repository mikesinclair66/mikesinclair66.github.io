Our Services

AMK is here to provide a personal touch and give hope to clients feeling drowned by debt or financial hardships. Our main goal is to educate our clients so as to avoid feelings suppressed by debts. We coach them on how to maintain, negotiate, and get out of debt.

Credit Rebuilding

If you have a lower credit score, it's never too late to rebuild your credit. At AMK, we will go over your credit report with you and give you insight on how you can catch up with your outstanding payments. On top of this, we will guide you on how to pay off your debt more quickly and give advice on how to better manage your bills in the future. Since your credit score plays a major factor in securing a loan for a mortgage or other large purchases, we can help you to get there!

Management Programs

AMK has the experience and knowledge to help you better manage your money and investments. We offer many different programs that will suit your personal financial needs. Contact us today to see how we can help you have a better understanding of how to manage your hard earned money.

Budget Management

Setting up, following, and managing a budget can be tricky, especially for major investments. The team at AMK will help you figure out the budget you can work with as well as take you through the steps of ensuring you stay within it. We can help you have better control of your budget which will greatly reduce any risks.

Credit Counselling

If you are looking to better understand your financial situation, AMK is here for you! Whether you have a poor credit rating, credit card debt, missed payments, or have been refused of a loan, we can professionally and objectively go through your situation and create a plan to help steer you in the right direction.

Financial Restructuring

At AMK, we offer financial restructuring programs for individuals and businesses. We know that sometimes you are too focused on the big picture to realize small details that may be affecting your profit and money flow. When you consult our staff, we can assist you by finding the underlying causes and help you restructure your financial plans to ensure nothing is missed.
